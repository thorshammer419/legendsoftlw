Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: May 2025

You have the right to request deletion of all personal data associated with your account in The Legends of TLW.

What gets deleted

  • Your player profile (name, email, notification settings)
  • Your character sheets across all campaigns
  • Your submitted actions and narrative history

How to request deletion

Send an email to erickson.mark.a@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request within 30 days and confirm via email when complete.

Removing Facebook login access

You can also revoke The Legends of TLW's access to your Facebook account at any time:

  1. Go to Facebook Settings → Security and Login → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "Legends of TLW" and click Remove

This revokes login access but does not automatically delete your game data. To delete game data, follow the email process above.
