Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

The Legends of TLW ("we", "us") is a private, invitation-only AI-assisted D&D storytelling app. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account information — your name and email address from your social login provider (Facebook or Microsoft).
  • Game data — character sheets, in-game actions, and narrative history you create while playing.

How We Use Your Information

  • To identify you within the app and link you to your characters and campaigns.
  • To send round notifications and campaign updates by email (only if you opt in).
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising.

Data Storage

All data is stored in Microsoft Azure (United States). Game narratives may be processed by Azure OpenAI to generate story content.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. See our Data Deletion page for instructions.
